Venue


The 25th IEEE International Conference on Network Protocols (ICNP 2017)

Contents

Chestnut Conference Centre, 89 Chestnut Street, Toronto, ON, M5G 1R1

Located adjacent to the city landmark of Yonge and Dundas Square and directly north of the city’s Financial District, Chestnut Conference Centre is in the heart of the downtown core. The venue is ideally located: it is only a 5-minute walk from the St Patrick subway station, 10-minute walk from the Eaton Centre and a 10-minute walk from Toronto’s Financial District.

Directions From Subway

From Bloor/Yonge Station or St. George Station, take the train southbound to Dundas Station or St. Patrick Station.

From Union Station, take the train northbound to St. Patrick Station or Dundas Station.

From Dundas Station, walking west on Dundas Street, Chestnut Street is at the 2nd set of lights west of Yonge Street. Time: 5 – 7 minute walk

From St. Patrick Station, walking east on Dundas Street, Chestnut is at the 1st set of lights east of University Avenue. Time: 2 – 5 minute walk

Directions From the Airport

Taxi/Limousine — Airport taxis and limousines are available at both Terminal 1 and Terminal 3. Travel time is approximately 45 minutes. You can also take the UP Express Trains to reach Union station, which takes about 15 minutes, and costs $12 CAD one-way.

Recommended Hotels

Since ICNP 2017 will be held at a conference centre, we do not have the notion of a "conference hotel." In early September, we have surveyed hotels in Downtown Toronto, and have the following recommendations. Pricing and availability are subject to changes.

Pricing will be more affordable if you venture further away from downtown. As long as you stay on the subway line, you can usually get to the conference centre within 20-30 minutes.
